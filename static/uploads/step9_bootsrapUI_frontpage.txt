what else can i use instead of 
card: gives a styled card container:
border – just adds a border (no padding/background)
bg-white p-4 rounded shadow – custom alternative without using card
alert alert-primary – more colorful box, meant for alerts
list-group – for stylized lists inside containers


text-center: centers text inside:
text-start – align left (default)
text-end – align right
text-md-center – center only on medium screens and above


p-5: adds padding inside the card (Bootstrap scale: 0–5):
Bootstrap padding scale: p-0 to p-5
You can also target specific sides:
pt-5 (top)
pb-5 (bottom)
px-5 (horizontal)
py-5 (vertical)
Use smaller values like p-3 or p-1 if too much space

shadow: gives a subtle drop shadow:
Variations:
shadow-sm – small shadow
shadow-lg – large shadow
shadow-none – remove shadow
